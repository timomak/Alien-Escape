Alien EscapeiOS Game
Alien Escape is a Puzzle solving/Strategy game that scores the user on his speed, accuracy and problem solving abilities. It allows you to take revenge on the aliens that have kidnapped you and brought you onto their spaceship. Use their own alien technology against them. Portals, vortexes and a Alien slingshot are all you have to surpass the increasingly difficult levels. As you progress, unlock new skins and items that will make all of your friends jealous of your achievements. Enjoy the difficulty and the satisfaction of passing from one level to another while taking out Aliens.
